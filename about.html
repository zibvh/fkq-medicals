About FKQ Medicals

Trusted medical diagnostics with a commitment to excellence and patient care

Welcome to FKQ Medical Diagnostic Hub Limited

A state-of-the-art medical diagnostic center dedicated to providing accurate, timely, and compassionate diagnostic services. Our mission is to deliver exceptional patient care, support medical practitioners with reliable results, and contribute to the wellbeing of our community.

Our Vision

To become the leading diagnostic hub in Nigeria, known for innovation, quality, and patient-centered care.

  • Preferred diagnostic partner in the region
  • Culture of innovation and excellence
  • Cutting-edge medical advancements

Our Mission

To provide accurate, reliable, and timely diagnostic services that improve healthcare outcomes.

  • Patient safety and satisfaction first
  • Continuous improvement and learning
  • Collaboration with healthcare providers

Our Values

Core principles that guide our operations and patient interactions.

  • Integrity: Honest and ethical practices
  • Excellence: Highest quality standards
  • Compassion: Empathetic patient care
  • Innovation: Adopting new technologies

Our History

2020

Foundation

FKQ Medical Diagnostic Hub was founded with a vision to provide quality diagnostic services in Ogbomoso and surrounding regions.

2021

Expansion

Added advanced hematology and biochemistry analyzers, expanding our test menu to over 50 different parameters.

2023

COVID-19 Response

Designated as a COVID-19 testing center, serving the community during the pandemic with rapid testing and vaccination.

2025

Modernization

Complete digital transformation with online reporting, appointment booking, and teleconsultation services.
